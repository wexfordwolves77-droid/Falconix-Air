Our Mission

Falconix Air is a Part 135 charter airline built for the next generation of regional travel. We specialize in short-haul, low-passenger flights with high-tech aircraft and AI-enhanced scheduling. Our goal: to make private aviation accessible, efficient, and carbon-conscious.
