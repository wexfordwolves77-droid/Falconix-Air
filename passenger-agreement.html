1. Definitions

For the purposes of this Agreement, “Carrier” refers to Falconix Air, “Passenger” refers to any person booked or boarded on a Falconix Air flight, and “Flight” refers to any scheduled or charter service operated by Falconix Air.

2. Applicability

This Agreement applies to all flights operated by Falconix Air, including scheduled, charter, and repositioning flights, and governs the rights and responsibilities of both the Carrier and the Passenger.

3. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

All flights are subject to applicable Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations, Transportation Security Administration (TSA) requirements, and any other relevant governmental authority.

4. Ticketing and Reservations

Passengers must present valid identification and a confirmed booking to board. Tickets are non-transferable unless otherwise stated in writing by Falconix Air.

5. Check-In and Boarding

Passengers must check in no later than 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes for international flights. Boarding gates close 10 minutes before scheduled departure.

6. Baggage Policy

Carry-on and checked baggage allowances are subject to weight and size restrictions. Prohibited items as defined by the TSA and FAA are not permitted onboard.

7. Dangerous Goods

Passengers are prohibited from carrying hazardous materials, including but not limited to explosives, flammable liquids, and compressed gases, unless specifically authorized under FAA regulations.

8. Passenger Conduct

Passengers must comply with crew instructions at all times. Disruptive, abusive, or unsafe behavior may result in removal from the flight and potential legal action.

9. Refusal of Transport

Falconix Air reserves the right to refuse transport to any Passenger who poses a safety risk, fails to comply with regulations, or is under the influence of drugs or alcohol.

11. Liability Limitations

Falconix Air’s liability for loss, damage, or delay of baggage is limited in accordance with applicable law. The Carrier is not liable for indirect or consequential damages.

12. Flight Delays and Cancellations

Schedules are subject to change without notice. Falconix Air will make reasonable efforts to notify Passengers of delays or cancellations and arrange alternative transportation when possible.

13. Force Majeure

Falconix Air is not liable for delays or cancellations caused by events beyond its control, including weather, air traffic control restrictions, strikes, or security incidents.

14. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United States and the state in which the flight originates.

15. Amendments

Falconix Air reserves the right to amend this Agreement at any time. Updated terms will be posted on the Carrier’s official website.

16. Passenger Identification

All Passengers must present valid government-issued photo identification prior to boarding. International Passengers must present a valid passport and any required visas or travel documents.

17. Security Screening

All Passengers and baggage are subject to security screening by the TSA or other authorized agencies. Refusal to submit to screening will result in denial of boarding without refund.

18. Seating Assignments

Falconix Air will make reasonable efforts to honor pre-selected seating assignments; however, the Carrier reserves the right to reassign seats at any time for operational or safety reasons.

19. Special Assistance

Passengers requiring special assistance, including wheelchair service or medical accommodations, must notify Falconix Air at least 48 hours prior to departure.

20. Unaccompanied Minors

Children under the age of 15 traveling alone must be booked under Falconix Air’s Unaccompanied Minor Program, with applicable fees and documentation requirements.

21. Pregnant Passengers

Passengers beyond 36 weeks of pregnancy may be required to present a medical certificate confirming fitness to fly. Falconix Air reserves the right to deny boarding if travel is deemed unsafe.

22. Medical Conditions

Passengers with medical conditions requiring in-flight oxygen, medication refrigeration, or other special equipment must coordinate with Falconix Air at least 72 hours prior to departure.

23. In-Flight Behavior

Use of threatening, abusive, or obscene language, as well as interference with crew duties, is strictly prohibited and may result in arrest upon landing.

24. Alcohol and Drug Policy

Passengers may not board or remain on board if intoxicated or under the influence of illegal substances. Consumption of personal alcohol on board is prohibited.

25. Electronic Devices

Use of portable electronic devices is permitted only in accordance with crew instructions and FAA regulations. Certain devices may be prohibited during takeoff and landing.

26. Wi-Fi and Connectivity

In-flight internet access is provided where available. Falconix Air is not responsible for service interruptions or data loss during use.

27. Pets and Service Animals

Small pets may travel in the cabin if secured in an approved carrier. Service animals are permitted in accordance with the Air Carrier Access Act (ACAA).

28. Emotional Support Animals

Emotional support animals are accepted only with proper documentation submitted at least 48 hours prior to departure, and must comply with size and behavior requirements.

29. Lost and Found

Items left on board will be held for 30 days. Falconix Air is not responsible for loss or damage to personal property left unattended.

30. Customer Feedback

Passengers may submit feedback or complaints via Falconix Air’s official website or customer service hotline. All submissions will be reviewed within 14 business days.
